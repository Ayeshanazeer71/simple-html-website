Ramadan Kareem

Welcome to our special Ramadan website. During this blessed month, Muslims worldwide observe fasting from dawn to sunset. This is a time of spiritual reflection, increased charity, and devotion.

Key Features of Ramadan:

  • Fasting from dawn to sunset
  • Special prayers (Taraweeh)
  • Increased charitable activities
  • Community iftars
  • Reading and understanding the Quran
